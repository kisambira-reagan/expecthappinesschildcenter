our mission

Founded in 2016, Our mission is to provide assistance and support to Orphans in a small village called Kapyanga located in Bugiri District.
Every child deserves love. Every widow deserves hope. And every person deserves a chance to smile again.
Whether you're here to donate, volunteer, or simply learn more, we welcome you with open arms.

our story

in the heart of Kapyanga where hope often feels distant, Expect Happiness Child Center was born to bring light towards the lives of those facing adversities. What started as a small initiative has grown into a beacon of hope for the most vulnerable members in our community.

who we are

Expect Happiness Child Center is a community- based-trusted charity organization devoted to caring for orphans in our small village. As a trusted charitable organization founded in 2018 by a group of dedicated individuals (Kisambira Reagan & Egesa Elly) with a shared vision of brighter tomorrow, this sanctuary has become a lifeline for many including highly struggling families within the community.

what we do

Our mission is to provide safe shelter, nourishing food, education, clothing, medication, emotional support, and a loving environment where 35 children can heal, grow, and thrive. Our work is rooted in compassion, community, and the belief that everyone deserves a chance to feel loved and be happy. We believe that every child deserves to be loved, seen and supported. Guided by compassion, community spirit, and faith.
we work daily to turn sorrow into smiles, and hardship into hope. So together, we are a beacon of hope lighting their way to a better tomorrow.
